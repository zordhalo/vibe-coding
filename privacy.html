Welcome to Vibe Coding Framework ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use your information. Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences (such as cookie consent choices).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., remembering your cookie consent preference).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information about you:

  • Email Service Provider: To manage our newsletter subscriptions and send emails.
  • Analytics: To understand website traffic and user behavior.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): To serve fonts, icons, and stylesheets (e.g., Google Fonts, Font Awesome, Bootstrap).

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or contacting us directly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Our website contains links to external websites (such as YouTube, LinkedIn, and the App Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
